To Do App

If you're having trouble using the app, please read the faqs below.

Guide:

  • Q: What is a folder, list and a task?
  • A: In the context of this app, a folder is a container for your lists and while a list a container for your tasks.
  • Q: Can I use this app to list anything aside from todo lists?
  • A: Of course! Do as you please. You can use whatever purpose it may serve.
  • Q: How to a folder?
  • A: It is very simple. Just open the sidemenu and click on the plus icon at the top of the sidemenu.
  • Q: How to a list?
  • A: Once you have succesfully created a folder, click on the folder and you will redirected to the folder page. Once there, you will see a note icon at the top right corner of the screen. Click that and the rest is easy.
  • Q: How to a task?
  • A: Similar to adding a list, just click on the list and you will redirected to tasks page. Once there, repeat the same process for adding a list.
  • Q: How to edit and delete lists and tasks
  • A: Simply swipe a list towards the left side of the screen and the options will be displayed.
  • Q: How to set a task to complete?
  • A. Just click on a task and it will be automatically set to complete.
