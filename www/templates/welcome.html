Welcome!

To Do App is a simple program that let's you add list of tasks and categorized them by folder.

Here's the flow:

  • Open the sidebar and add a folder.
  • Click the folder and add a list.
  • Click the list and tasks to it.

Features:

  • Adding, Editing, Deleting of folder/categories.
  • Adding, Editing, Deleting of lists.
  • Adding, Editing, Deleting of tasks.
